Visit Us

Off-Peak Season (September-February)

  1. Timed passes will only be required on weekends.
  2. Passes are available online three months in advance and are issued the first Wednesday of every month.
  3. Same-day online passes are released at 6:30 am, Saturday and Sunday.
  4. Groups are required to have passes every day during peak and off-peak seasons.
  5. Walk-up entry is not available on Saturdays or Sundays due to high visitation.

Peak Season (March-August)

  1. Walk-Up Weekday entry begins at 1pm Monday through Friday.
  2. Passes are available online three months in advance and are issued the first Wednesday of every month.
  3. Same-day online passes are released at 6:30 am, Monday – Sunday.
  4. Groups are required to have passes every day during peak and off-peak seasons.
  5. Walk-up entry is not available on Saturdays or Sundays due to high visitation.

Exchanges and Cancellations

Free passes may not be sold. If you cannot visit the museum on your scheduled date and cannot find someone who can use them, then you may cancel your passes or exchange them for a different date, subject to availability. Please initiate your exchange or cancel request by submitting an Etix customer service request. Include your order number and requested dates and times if applicable.

Security

For everyone’s safety, all visitors are required to pass through screening at the entrance to the Museum. During the security process, you are greeted by one of our security personnel who conduct a thorough but speedy hand-check of all bags, briefcases, purses, strollers, and containers. All visitors are required to walk through a metal detector. Those visitors unable to go through the metal detector will be hand-screened with an electronic wand by security personnel. Please see a list of items not allowed in the Museum before planning your visit.
